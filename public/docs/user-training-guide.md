# Infinity Hotel PMS — User Training Guide

Version 1.0 · For all staff roles

---

## 1. Getting started

1. Open the app URL provided by your administrator.
2. Click **Sign in** and enter your work email + password. Use Google if your admin enabled it.
3. On first login you may be asked to accept an invite and select a property.
4. The top-right avatar menu lets you switch **Property** if you work at more than one hotel.

## 2. Understanding your role

Every user is assigned one or more roles (Front Desk, Housekeeping, Manager, etc.). Your role controls which menu items you see and what actions you can perform. If a button is greyed out or hidden, your role does not permit that action — contact your admin.

## 3. Daily front-desk workflow

1. **Reservations → New reservation** — pick dates, room type, guest.
2. **Check-in** from the reservation detail page. Attach guest ID.
3. **Assign a room** from the room grid; status turns to Occupied.
4. **Post charges** during stay (POS orders, minibar, laundry).
5. **Check-out** — settle balance via Payments, then release the room to Housekeeping.

## 4. Housekeeping

- Open **Housekeeping** to see today's clean / dirty / inspected room list.
- Tap a room to change status. Supervisors can bulk-assign attendants.

## 5. Point of Sale (POS)

1. Open **POS** and pick your outlet.
2. Create an order, add menu items, send KOT to kitchen.
3. Take payment (cash, card, room charge). Room charges post to the guest folio automatically.

## 6. Reports & analytics

- **Reports** — nightly audit, occupancy, revenue, ADR, RevPAR.
- **Analytics** — trend dashboards and scheduled exports (CSV/PDF).
- Use the **Export** menu on any list page to download CSV or PDF.

## 7. Notifications

- The bell icon shows real-time alerts (new bookings, low stock, failed syncs).
- Admins can purge online users from **Notifications → Purge online users**.

## 8. Security & good practice

- Never share your password. Enable 2-factor auth from **Settings → Security**.
- Log out at the end of your shift.
- Report suspicious files or emails through **Admin → Security Center → File Firewall**.

## 9. Getting help

- In-app: click the **?** icon in the top bar for context help.
- Contact your property admin or email the support address in **Settings → Brand**.
